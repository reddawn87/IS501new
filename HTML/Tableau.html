The above image describes how profits are doing in each state. We can see that the state that makes the most profit is California and the state that lost the most on profits is Texas. Overall, profits are positive while some states are in the negatives and are actually loosing money. Further analysis will detail how California is so successful.



For the past 2 years, California has had a series of booms and busts. This suggests that the needs of consumers change quite frequently. It is apparent that analysts are able to react quickly to market changes and push the most popular product. When the trend goes down, changes are made to bring the profit margins back up. We do however reccomend that something be done to smooth out the profits.



Of the three product categories in California, office supplies is by far the most sucessful. Techonology is the second most sucessful. Hardly any of the profit is made from furiniture. We reccomend that less furiniture be produced to boost sales in orther categories.



Due to the boom and bust cycles in California, it is dificult to produce super accurate forcasting. However, it is clear that slow and stead growth can be expected for total sales. There is a big potential for sales to be very large or very small, historical averages however suggest an overall increase.
